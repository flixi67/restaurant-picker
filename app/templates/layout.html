How to Use the Restaurant Matching App

  1. Click ‘New Meeting’ to start a new session. Set the group size, preferred date, and time. You will receive a unique Meeting ID.
  2. Go to ‘Add Member’ and enter the Meeting ID. Fill in your preferences:
    • Your location
    • Maximum budget
    • Minimum restaurant rating
    • Importance ranking (1–3) for:
      • Budget
      • Rating
      • Closeness of the restaurant
    • Payment method (Card, Cash)
    • Dietary preference (Vegetarian)
  3. Once all members have submitted their preferences, go to ‘Get Results’, enter the Meeting ID, and view the restaurant recommendations customized for your group.
